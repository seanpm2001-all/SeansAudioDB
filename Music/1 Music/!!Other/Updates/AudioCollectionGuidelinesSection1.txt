Section 1 - Download (almost) everything

With the audio collection, the goal is to download every piece of music ever listened to by Sean (that can be found) and for sound effects, there are different guidelines.
Significant/comedic sound effects and easy to grab onez are downloaded, but things like peoples voices, custom animal noises (voices of personal pets, and friends pets) are not a priority.
