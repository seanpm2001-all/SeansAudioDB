My first Bardcore - June 23rd 2020 [Audio collection notes]

Earlier at around 1:00-2:00 am this morning, I was browsing Reddit and came across a post on r/youshouldknow about a recent trend on YouTube where people have been composing various popular songs with medieval instruments, so I decided to go check it out.
The music category is called Bardcore, and I really like it, I have always liked songs with an ancient/old theme to them. This is a trend I really like. I watched 10 songs before bed, and later listened to 23 more songs in the morning, along with replaying some of the original 10 songs.

Since my music collection archives everything I listen to, I began to download these songs later in the day. The only problems I currently have is that I can't combine the audio and video tracks yet, and also because 1 song was really difficult and wouldn't download in full, as my downloader just couldn't do it, which is the Undertale - Megalovania Bardcore remix.

This trend is very new, it apparently originated on April 20th 2020.

I now plan on downloading versions of my music in different styles now, so that a single song can have many variations. This will take some time and I won't be able to do this to every song.
