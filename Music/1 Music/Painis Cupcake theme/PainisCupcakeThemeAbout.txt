About the painis cupcake theme

Painis cupcake is a parody character from the Team Fortress 2 franchise created in 2010 by the YouTube channel rubberfruit.
He is based off the soldier class, and his key line is "I am pain is cupcake. I will eat you" proceeding with a creepy smile

This character has a theme in videos which is from Donkey Kong [version]
The original song is known as "Puzzles in the caves"
